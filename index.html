Hi - I’m Laban! I’m just a guy trying to find himself. As someone who loves new challenges, I’ve found myself struggling to pinpoint how I want to spend the majority of my time. When I graduate, where should I work? What should I build?

So far, my path has been nothing if not meandering. I’ve run a hackathon, I’ve worked a summer in finance, and I've just completed an internship at a management consulting firm. Because I don’t know what the future has in store, this website is a way for me to preserve lessons I've learned. Likely, none of these will be groundbreaking - rather, perhaps obvious - but it helps to recall how I learned those lessons.

If any of this resonates with you, let’s get in touch: you can find me at hello@labanl.in. Cheers!
